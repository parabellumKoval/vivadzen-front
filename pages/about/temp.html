About us

We started modestly: we looked for reliable suppliers, tested the first batches and built a philosophy based on honesty, transparency, quality and responsibility.

Over time, our team grew, and with it, our product range. We discovered the unique properties of herbal teas, organic coffee, hemp products, superfoods and medicinal mushrooms. We develop each of these areas with special attention to detail – from the place of cultivation to certification and independent laboratory tests.

In 2022, we took another important step – we opened our first store in the heart of Prague. This became a symbol of our philosophy: combining quality, openness and love for nature with care for our customers. Today, we are already expanding beyond the Czech Republic and increasing our presence in Europe by opening branches in Spain and Germany.

VivaDzen began its journey in 2012 with a simple but important goal: to make high-quality natural products accessible to people, helping them to take care of their health, harmony and balance in everyday life.
